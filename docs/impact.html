Launch Event at the Environmental Interpretive Center

Robin's first-ever app launch on campus: tested, demoed, and celebrated by the community.

On April 14, 2025, we hosted Robin's official launch at the EIC. The event brought together over 60 attendees, including Provost Gabriella Scarlatta, Dean Ghassan Kridli, CIS Chair Dr. Jie Shen, and our project supervisor Dr. Zheng Song, along with professors like Dr. Sirja Das and Professor Bruce Maxim. Students, faculty, friends, family, and visitors from across campus joined us to experience Robin firsthand.

Q&A Session

The launch featured live testing and demos of Robin, with attendees exploring our research, picking up merchandise, and providing valuable feedback. A highlight was the participation of Donna Posont, a visually impaired guest who helped test the app’s accessibility features live. Her feedback was instrumental in shaping Robin’s voice control, spoken descriptions, and overall usability.

Special thanks to Dr. Jacob Napieralski and the EIC staff for their support and guidance in making Robin a reality.
