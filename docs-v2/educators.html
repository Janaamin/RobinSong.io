Robin x GLSI

We’re building something exciting for educators across Michigan and beyond.

RobinRoots Initiative

RobinRoots is a growing initiative powered by Robin in collaboration with the Great Lakes Stewardship Initiative (GLSI) and passionate teachers statewide. Together, we’re co-creating lesson plans, classroom tools, and outdoor learning experiences that make birding accessible, meaningful, and fun for all types of learners.

Stay tuned — we’ll be sharing educator resources, curriculum-aligned materials, and partnership opportunities right here soon!
