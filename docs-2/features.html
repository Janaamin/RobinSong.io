Key Features

Discover how Robin's innovative features make birdwatching accessible and enjoyable for everyone.

Real-Time Sound ID

Identify birds in real-time using BirdNET, with multi-species detection and an average detection speed of less than 3 seconds.

ChatGPT Bird Expert

Ask "Why is it singing?" and get accurate, source-verified answers about diet, behavior, and migration with voice input and text-to-speech replies.

Predictive Forecasts

Plan outings with 87% hotspot accuracy using monthly predictions tailored to your local area, based on five years of eBird data.

Text-to-Speech

Experience hands-free, inclusive navigation that's seamless with VoiceOver & TalkBack, narrating all bird data automatically.

Bird Log History

Keep an automatic, searchable journal of every identification, complete with species, date, time, and GPS location.

Inclusive Design

Built for accessibility with high-contrast visuals, large-text options, haptic feedback, and streamlined navigation.

Cross-Platform

Enjoy a consistent, accessible experience on both iOS & Android, built with React Native for smooth performance.

Why Robin Stands Out

Robin unites intelligent tech, ecology, and inclusive design to let everyone enjoy nature through sound.
