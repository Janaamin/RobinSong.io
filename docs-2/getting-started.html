App Permissions Explained

Understanding how Robin uses your device features—transparently and responsibly

Microphone Access

Robin needs microphone access to identify birds by their sounds. This is essential for the core bird identification feature.

Audio is processed locally on your device and never stored without your explicit permission.

  • Real-time bird sound analysis
  • Voice-guided identification
  • Local audio processing

Location Access

Location access helps Robin provide more accurate bird forecasts for your specific area and improves identification accuracy.

Your location data stays on your device and is never shared with third parties.

  • Local bird forecasts
  • Seasonal migration alerts
  • Enhanced identification

Continuous listening captures complete bird calls for instant identification. Audio is processed locally and never stored without your explicit consent.
